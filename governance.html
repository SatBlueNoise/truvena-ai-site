The Truvena Governance System

A practical, mission‑aligned framework for responsible AI adoption.

Our Framework

The Truvena Governance System is built around five pillars that ensure AI is used responsibly, ethically, and safely.

  • Risk & Compliance: Legal, operational, and reputational safeguards.
  • Responsible AI: Ethical alignment with mission and community impact.
  • Operational Readiness: Processes, workflows, and controls.
  • Oversight & Accountability: Clear roles, reporting, and decision rights.
  • Continuous Improvement: Monitoring, auditing, and iteration.
