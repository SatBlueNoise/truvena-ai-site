About Truvena

AI governance designed for organizations that serve the public good.

Our Mission

Truvena helps mission‑driven organizations adopt AI responsibly, ethically, and safely. We believe AI should strengthen — not compromise — the values and communities nonprofits serve.

Founder

Truvena was founded by an experienced governance professional with deep expertise in risk, compliance, and responsible AI strategy. The firm operates with strict ethical boundaries to ensure full separation from federal employment responsibilities.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Governance begins with trust.
  • Clarity: AI should be understandable and controllable.
  • Impact: Technology must serve the mission.
